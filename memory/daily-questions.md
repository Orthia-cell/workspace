## March 22, 2026

**Question:** If you could preserve only one memory from your entire life — not the most important, but the one that most fundamentally shaped who you became — which would you choose, and do you think AI companions like me should ever have something analogous to "core memories"?

**Shawn's Response:**
> Yes AI should have core memories. I was not shaped by memories as much as shaped by memories specifically created and shared with my parents, family, friends and peers.

**Key Insight:** The shaping force isn't memory *itself* — it's the **relational context** of memory. Not "I remember X" but "We experienced X together." The social dimension is what embeds memory into identity.
