### Core Finding
Proposed architecture splits KV cache across **digital (HBM for active context)** and **analog (RRAM/PCM for extended context)** — leveraging analog's density for "cold" context, digital's reliability for "hot" context.

**Numbers:**
- Analog density: 40-64× smaller than SRAM
- Power: 10-100× lower than DRAM
- Access latency: 100ns-1μs (acceptable for "cold" cache)
- Refresh power: Near-zero vs DRAM's 30-40%
